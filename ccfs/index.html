SCHEME OVERVIEW

One-Time Amnesty Opportunity

The CCFS-2026 is designed by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) to offer defaulting companies a fresh start. It provides an exclusive window to clear overdue annual returns and financial statements without facing the typical massive additional fees.

  • Active Period: April 15, 2026 to July 15, 2026
  • Immunity: Protection from prospective penal actions
  • Objective: Improved accuracy of the corporate registry

KEY BENEFITS & RELIEF

Pending Filings

File overdue annual returns and financial statements by paying the normal statutory fee plus only 10% of the applicable additional fees.

Dormant Status

Inactive companies can apply for "Dormant" status under Section 455 by filing e-form MSC-1 at a heavily discounted 50% of the normal filing fee.

Voluntary Strike-off

Companies wishing to close down cleanly can apply for voluntary strike-off by filing e-form STK-2 at just 25% of the applicable fee.

ELIGIBLE FORMS

The scheme covers essential e-forms under both the Companies Act, 2013, and the Companies Act, 1956. Ensure your compliance is up to date.

FORM NUMBER PURPOSE / FILING DOCUMENT
MGT-7 & MGT-7A Filing Annual Returns (Standard & Small Companies)
AOC-4 Financial Statements (including CFS and XBRL versions)
ADT-1 Information to the Registrar about Auditor appointment
FC-3 & FC-4 Annual accounts/returns of Foreign Companies

NON-ELIGIBILITY

The scheme provides broad coverage, but specific companies are excluded from these benefits.

Not Applicable For:

  • Companies facing a final notice for striking off (Section 248) issued by the RoC.
  • Companies that have already actively applied for voluntary strike-off.
  • Companies that have already obtained dormant status.
  • Companies dissolved via corporate amalgamation.
  • Vanishing Companies.

Important Notice

After the scheme concludes on July 15, 2026, Registrars of Companies are mandated to initiate necessary regulatory actions against companies that remain in default.
